History

The NYCU Department of Computer Science was established in 1970. Through multiple reforms and advancements in technology, it has become one of Taiwan's leading CS departments.

Faculty

The department consists of over 50 professors and researchers, dedicated to various fields of research and teaching, providing students with a high-quality learning environment.

Research Areas

Research areas cover artificial intelligence, data science, embedded systems, and other cutting-edge fields, continually driving technological innovation.

Achievements

Many alumni hold key positions in international tech companies and have made significant breakthroughs and contributions in the academic world.
