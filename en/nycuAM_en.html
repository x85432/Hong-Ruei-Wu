History

The NYCU Department of Applied Mathematics was established in 1994, aiming to cultivate students with a solid mathematical foundation and applied skills, and to promote interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation.

Faculty

The department has over 30 full-time professors covering pure mathematics, applied mathematics, computational mathematics, and other fields, actively participating in international collaborations and academic exchanges.

Research Areas

Research areas include discrete mathematics, data science, statistics, numerical analysis, and optimization theory, maintaining close collaboration with leading universities worldwide.

Achievements

Our graduates are widely represented in academia and industry, with many alumni holding important positions in internationally renowned companies and research institutions, achieving significant accomplishments in mathematics and technology fields.
